The Hamburger Company

Since 1963

The Hamburger Company is a family owned and run restaurant, takeaway and bar and event space located in the heart of Barnsley. Since it's inception in 1963, The Hamburger Company has become an institution amongst generations who have grown up with the restaurant and recognise it for its high standards, suburb quality and gracious hospitality.

Open and serving food all day from noon ‘til late Monday to Sunday, The Hamburger Company offers diners a truly inspiring traditional Italian menu. As well as the A La Carte menu, The Hamburger Company also offers a selection of Chef’s Daily Specials, a dedicated Gluten Free menu, Kids menu and Celebration party menus.
